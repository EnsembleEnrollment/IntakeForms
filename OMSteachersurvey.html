Photo Guidelines
  1. Photo must have been taken within the last two years
  2. Choose a neutral background (e.g., white wall) — not your car, bathroom, or bedroom
  3. Smile and look directly at the camera. Don't angle from above or below
  4. Ideally waist-up so your face is clearly visible
  5. Please don't take a selfie — have someone else take it or set up 5–10 steps away
  6. Make sure the photo is not blurry
  7. You should be the only person in the photo
